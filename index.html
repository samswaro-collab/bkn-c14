🏢 Company Description
Neon Aerospace is an AI-native, early-stage Tier-1 aerospace supplier and startup founded in January 2025. Based in Irvine, CA with a 10,000 sq ft R&D facility, Neon designs and manufactures advanced flight systems for the electrified aviation market.

Core Products: Electro-mechanical actuation platform, composite propellers and rotors, smart actuators, lightweight flight systems for eVTOL and advanced air mobility platforms.

Market Position: Neon targets eVTOL/AAM, advanced air mobility, and defense/commercial aircraft OEMs (Archer Aviation, Joby, Wisk, etc.). The company positions itself as a digitally-integrated, compliance-ready alternative to legacy adversarial supplier relationships that characterize traditional aerospace.

Leadership: Founded by Dave Dennison (CEO, former VP Operations at Archer, VP Eng at Bell) and Adam Newton (Engineering Lead, former Director Engineering at Archer). The founding team includes senior actuation engineers with 15+ years experience at Boeing, UTC, and Overair.

Services Offered:
1. Design & Engineering — conceptual design through certification
2. Production & Manufacturing — composite fabrication, tooling, sourcing
3. Validation & Assurance — testing, instrumentation, regulatory compliance

Funding & Stage: Pre-revenue, seed stage. Funding not publicly disclosed. Actively hiring for systems engineering, AI/ML, data platforms, and manufacturing roles.
